Agriculture — On The Job

Air movement that protects livestock

HVLS fans help with animal welfare and performance by reducing heat stress and keeping bedding and floors drier. We install over auction barns, show arenas, dairy and livestock buildings, and other ag spaces, working around the open structure these buildings usually have.

We're brand-independent and service whatever's already hanging, so you get the fan that fits your building.

    The short version

  • Reduces heat stress on livestock
  • Drier bedding, floors & surfaces
  • Moves odor and stale air out
  • Fits open barn & arena structures
